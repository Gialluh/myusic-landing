Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

MYUSIC is a social music platform. This policy explains how we handle your data on our website myusic.app.

What we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address. That's it. We don't collect cookies, tracking data, or any other personal information on this website.

Why we collect it

To notify you when MYUSIC launches and to send occasional updates about the app. Nothing else.

Where your data is stored

Your email is stored securely in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the MYUSIC team.

Who sees your data

Only the MYUSIC founding team. We never sell, share, or give your email to anyone else.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete your email at any time by contacting us at hello@myusic.app.

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here with an updated date.
