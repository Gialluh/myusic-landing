Myusic is a space where music moves between people who share the same rhythm. Quality over quantity. Tracks are passed between like-minded individuals, without intermediaries. No background noise, just real connections.

I heard a song last night that made me want to call everyone I've ever loved.

I didn't. But I shared it. And someone on the other side felt the exact same thing.

Connection is not a Wi-Fi problem.

It's a timing problem. You just have to be in the same place, at the same time, listening to the same thing.

The world is basically a room full of people all playing different songs at the same time.

And somehow it sounds alright. Not good. But alright. And alright is underrated.

How it works

01

Drop

Share one song per cycle. Wednesday to Sunday, Sunday to Wednesday. One choice, make it count.

02

Sessions

Someone shares a song. You reply with a song. A conversation starts — no words needed.

03

Mood Requests

Ask your friends for the right song for the moment. "Songs for a rooftop at sunset" — they answer with music.

04

Radar

Discover music from people outside your circle. Public drops land here. New taste, new connections.

05

Your Meusic

Three songs that define you. Prompts that reveal your taste. Your profile is your music identity.

06

Listen Later

Save songs that catch your ear. 10 slots. 30 days. Then they're gone. Listen now or lose them.
